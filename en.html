Dipedra

A company based in Bahia, Brazil, dedicated to the ornamental stone industry for over 30 years. It operates across the entire value chain, from the extraction of exotic stones to the delivery of high-end projects.

Specializing in marble, granite, and quartzite, Dipedra serves as the link between nature and high-end architecture. From large-scale supply for construction companies to the detailed execution of exclusive projects, we deliver tailored solutions for those who demand the highest standards of quality and durability in the natural stone segment.

Our mission is to transform natural stones into outstanding projects with punctuality, quality, sophistication, and functionality, always providing personalized attention to clients and partners in both domestic and international markets.

From mining to the production of ornamental pieces, Dipedra stands for expertise, trust, tradition, refined taste, and quality.
